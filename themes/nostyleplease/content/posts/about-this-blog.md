# what is this place?

this is where i dump whatever's in my head. no tech stuff. no tutorials. no trying to sound smart.

just me being... human? maybe. or maybe documenting the descent into madness. who knows.

some days i'll rant about random shit. some days i'll be at my lowest, typing through the chaos. some days i might actually make sense.

this is my outlet. my documentation. my proof that i existed and felt things.

raw. unfiltered. probably messy.
